We recommend booking at least 2–3 months in advance for bridal services, especially during the peak wedding season (November to February and April to June). Early booking ensures your preferred date and allows time for trial sessions.
Yes! Complete bridal setup including saree draping, jewellery setting, flower decoration, veil/net setting, and eyelash fixing are all available as add-on services or included in our bridal packages. Please mention your requirements when booking.
For a complete bridal session (makeup + hair + draping), the typical duration is 3–5 hours. Airbrush and Ultra HD makeup may take slightly longer. We recommend scheduling the session at least 4–5 hours before the muhurtham time.
