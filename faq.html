💄 Makeup Services

Yes! Saree draping and jewellery setting are part of our complete bridal service. We don't just do makeup — we prepare the full bridal look from head to toe.

Our bridal setup includes:

  • 🥻 Saree Draping — Perfect pleat and pallu draping, traditional South Indian style or modern style
  • 💍 Jewellery Setting — Proper placement and securing of all jewellery pieces
  • 🌸 Flower Decoration — Jasmine, rose or custom flowers in hair
  • 🫛 Veil / Net Setting — Bridal net and dupatta placement
  • 👁️ Eyelash Fixing — Natural or dramatic false lashes
  • 💅 Nail Styling — Nail polish and nail art

These may be included in packages or available as add-ons. Please mention your requirements when booking.

Bridal makeup typically takes around 3 hours for the complete session. Here is a general breakdown:

  • 🕐 Makeup (Face + Eyes + Lips): 1.5 – 2 hours depending on the type (Traditional, HD, Ultra HD or Airbrush)
  • 💇‍♀️ Hair Styling: 30 – 45 minutes
  • 🥻 Saree Draping + Jewellery Setting: 20 – 30 minutes
  • 🌸 Final touches (flowers, lashes, etc.): 15 minutes

Total: Approximately 3 – 3.5 hours for a complete bridal setup. We recommend starting the session at least 3.5 to 4 hours before your muhurtham time so everything is relaxed and perfect with no rushing.

Ultra HD and Airbrush makeup may take a little longer depending on complexity.

🌿 Mehndi

Yes — of course! Completely your choice. We always welcome and respect custom mehndi design requests.

You can:

  • 📸 Share a reference photo — from Pinterest, Instagram or Google
  • 🎨 Describe your idea — theme, style, elements you love (flowers, peacocks, mango patterns, initials, etc.)
  • 💡 Mix and match — combine elements from multiple designs into one unique pattern
  • ✍️ Ask for custom name or initials — we can incorporate your name or groom's name into the design

We have 1000+ mehndi designs in our collection — Bridal, Arabic, Rajasthani, Mughal, Floral, Mandala, Indo-Western, Moroccan and more. Whatever you choose, our mehndi artist will create it beautifully just for you.

Full bridal mehndi (both hands and both legs with heavy intricate patterns) typically takes 4 to 6 hours depending on the complexity of the design chosen.

We recommend scheduling bridal mehndi the day before the wedding so it has time to develop a deep, rich dark colour by the wedding morning. The colour deepens overnight with natural heat.

🌿 After application, please keep the mehndi on for at least 6–8 hours for the best colour. We will give you full aftercare instructions to ensure deep dark staining.
